Privacy notice

How inquiry information is handled.

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Information we receive

When you submit a project inquiry, we receive the contact details, company information, project context, service preferences, and any document you choose to attach.

Why we use it

We use this information to review your request, contact you, prepare an appropriate next step, protect the inquiry form from misuse, and maintain a record of the conversation.

Storage and sharing

Inquiry information is stored in the website's protected inquiry system and may be delivered through the configured email provider. It is not sold. Access should be limited to people involved in responding to the request.

Your choices

You may ask to review, correct, or delete inquiry information by contacting hello@nexora.example.

Deployment note

Before launch, replace the sample company name, email, domain, retention period, and any provider-specific details with the final business information and applicable legal requirements.
